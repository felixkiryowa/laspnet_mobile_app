About Us

The justice system in Uganda is faced with a severe case backlog, which has negative consequences for poor and vulnerable persons.The lengthy adjournments and costs make poor people abandon their cases hence leading to destitution and increasing their ill-being. This Mobile Application provides case information to both the public and Judiciary to help reduce incidents of corruption within the justice system.

This is achieved by:

 Providing information to the public on how to file cases; both civil and criminal. Case tracking and filtering. Live-streaming of court proceedings. Displaying Cause Listings Judiciary Inspectorate Complaint Management. Viewing Hot Cases (Cases with the most hits) Help/FAQs/Contact Us Pages. Link to Laspnet IIMS
